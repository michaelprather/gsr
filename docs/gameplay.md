# Game Rules & Instructions

George Street Rummy is a multi-round rummy-style card game where the goal is to finish the game with the lowest total score.

## Cards & Dealing

	•	Standard deck including Jokers
	•	Each player is dealt 11 cards at the start of each round
	•	Jokers are wild
	•	Aces are high cards

## Round Objectives

Each round has a specific objective involving books and/or runs:
	•	Book: At least three cards of the same number
	•	Run: At least four sequential cards of the same suit

Rounds must be played in order and include:
	•	2 books
	•	1 book 1 run
	•	2 runs
	•	2 books 1 run
	•	2 runs 1 book
	•	3 books
	•	3 runs and out

## Winning a Round

	•	A player wins a round by playing all cards from their hand
	•	Typically, this occurs by discarding a final card
	•	In the final round (“3 runs and out”), all cards must be played immediately without discarding

## Scoring

When a player goes out, all other players score points based on the cards remaining in their hand:

Card Type	Points
Number cards (2–9) =	5
Tens & face cards =	10
Aces =	15
Jokers =	25

	•	The round winner always scores 0
	•	Total game score is the sum of all round scores
	•	Lowest total score wins the game
